Omars Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

Ingredients needed

  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla essence

Steps for creating cookie

  1. Melt butter until brown
  2. Mix sugar with butter
  3. Beat eggs with Vanilla essence so there is no smell of eggs
  4. Heat oven to 180 degree celius and bake for 10 mins until the sides start getting brown
